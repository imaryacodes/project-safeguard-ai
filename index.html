Research References
P1
Graham, P. (2002) — A Plan for Spam
Original paper proposing Bayesian spam filtering. Foundation of modern spam detection systems.
P2
Metsis et al. (2006) — Spam Filtering with Naive Bayes
Compares MultinomialNB vs BernoulliNB. Recommends TF-IDF + MultinomialNB pipeline used in this project.
P3
Almeida et al. (2011) — SMS Spam Filtering
Introduces the SMS Spam Collection dataset (5,574 messages) used to train SafeGuard AI.
P4
Sahami et al. (1998) — Bayesian Approach to Junk Email
Establishes Laplace smoothing (alpha=1.0) as critical for preventing overfitting in Naive Bayes classifiers.
P5
Sculley & Cormack (2007) — Filtering Spam in the Real World
Recommends cross-validation and feature limits to prevent overfitting — both applied in this project.
P6
Rennie et al. (2003) — Tackling Poor Assumptions of Naive Bayes
Explains why TF-IDF combined with Naive Bayes significantly improves text classification accuracy.
